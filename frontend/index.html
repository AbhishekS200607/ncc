About the Unit

Carmel Polytechnic College NCC prepares students for disciplined public service.

The NCC unit gives students a structured environment to grow beyond the classroom. Cadets develop punctuality, teamwork, leadership, physical endurance, and civic responsibility.

Training includes drill, parade, social service, fitness sessions, camps, awareness programs, and certificate-oriented preparation under NCC norms.

FAQ

Common enrollment questions

Who can apply for Carmel Polytechnic College NCC?

Eligible students currently studying at Carmel Polytechnic College can apply through the online enrollment form.

What happens after I submit the form?

The NCC unit reviews your application and contacts shortlisted students for the next enrollment steps.

Do I need to be physically fit?

Yes. NCC involves regular physical training, drill, and outdoor activities, so basic fitness and commitment are important.
